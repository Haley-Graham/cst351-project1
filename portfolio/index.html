Project 1

Design and implement a website that organizes and archives your weekly work for the semester. Website communicates your personal style, your professionalism, and your ability to design and implement a coherent system. This is the first project of the class, a Personal Portfolio website created with HTML and CSS. You will be designing a portfolio website for your partner, and they will be designing a portfolio website for you. In this exercise, you will both work together on a mind-map for each of you. While your partner will be designing your portfolio website with your guidance, you will provide the contents.

Project 2

In this half semester-long project you will apply the theories and principles of web design explored in the class to implement your designs using HTML, CSS, and Wordpress. Design and produce a working prototype of a website for a local organization. Using peer-generated content and systems design thinking, create a site that demonstrates your personal “style” while appropriately serving your client and target audience. You will create branding, color systems, typography, information architecture, image treatments, website specifications, and mock-ups in separate assignments that will all come together for your first prototype.
